Kiwis In the autumn season, kiwis are full of branches, which make people mouth watering. The shape of the kiwi is oval, the skin is tan, covered with small spots, and the fine fluff is soft and soft to touch. Gently peel off its skin to reveal the jasper-like flesh inside. The texture is like a folding fan that is opened, and it looks like a bow that is pulled up one by one, and it is covered with "small sesame seeds". Try it, it tastes sweet and refreshing. Kiwi is not only delicious, but also has high medicinal and nutritional value. It is said that kiwi is still a good medicine to prevent cancer!
