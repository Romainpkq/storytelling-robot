Summer night is quiet Summer night, the moonlight shone into the courtyard. Under the big tree, an old grandfather was enjoying himself. Not far from the old man, there is a little cat. After a while, the grandfather raised his hand and holding the trunk, slowly stood up, stretched himself, and walked into the room. A child was asleep in the room, and beside the child, there was a picture that had already been drawn.
