How to grow summer vegetables in winter In winter, the weather is cold. In order to eat summer vegetables, people build greenhouses for these vegetables. General greenhouses use glass or plastic films for walls and roofs to shield them from the wind and rain, and to absorb sunlight. In addition, they need to be heated to increase the temperature. If there is not enough light, install an "artificial sun". In this way, despite the midwinter years, people can still cultivate summer vegetables.
