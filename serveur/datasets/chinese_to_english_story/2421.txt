Spring silkworms are career-minded, and Madam Curie is someone who devotes her entire body and mind to the cause of science and human progress. She devoted her life to researching radium, establishing a new radiology science, and becoming a pioneer in nuclear physics. For the noble cause, work carefully and patiently, and dedicate everything to the people. This is the character of the spring silkworm. This is the character of the moving Mrs. Curie.
