Squirrel Squirrel is a beautiful little animal. It has flexible limbs and agile movements, always jumping on branches, chasing and playing with each other. On the delicate little face, a pair of sparkling little eyes were embedded, turning cautiously. The grayish-brown hair on the body is as smooth as oil, and it feels delicate and soft to the touch.
