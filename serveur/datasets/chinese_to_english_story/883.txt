The Olympic flag is white in color and the five central rings are arranged in two rows. The colors of the top three rings are blue, black, and red, respectively; the colors of the bottom two rings are yellow and green, respectively. The connection of the five rings symbolizes the people of the world holding hands and connecting hearts. The purpose of the Olympic Games is: faster, higher and stronger.
