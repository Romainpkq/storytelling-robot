Here comes the wolf Once upon a time, a child often raised sheep on the mountain. One day, he shouted down the mountain, "The wolf is here! The wolf is here!" When people ran up the mountain to rescue him, he smiled and said, "Without a wolf, I lied to you!" Lied people again. On this day, the wolf really came. The sheep-raising child shouted in shock: "The wolf is here! The wolf is here!" But no one came to save him. As a result, the wolf killed several sheep. Since then, he never lied again.
