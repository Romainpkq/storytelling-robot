A cruise ship was running on the water, with white waves rolled up on both sides of the bow, and a long ripple was dragged at the stern. Xiao Jie sits on the bow. She was eating bread while enjoying the scenery on both sides. Look, the mountain over there is like a frog about to jump, the mountain over here is like an eagle with wings spread out, the rock rabbits and rock turtles on the mountainside seem to be racing.
