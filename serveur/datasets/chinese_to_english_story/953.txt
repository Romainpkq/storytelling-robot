There is an old tailor in tailoring, and his business is particularly good in this shop. Once, a new official came to the store, and said with great vigor: "Make a suit for me!" The old tailor looked at the official, measured him, and cut the front of the clothes longer than the back. .... The official said angrily, "How to wear this kind of clothes?" The old tailor ignored him. Wait for the clothes to be ready, and the officer will just wear them! The old tailor smiled and said, "You have just become an officer, and you must walk with your chest up, so I made the front longer."
