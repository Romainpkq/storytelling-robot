Flying a kite On Saturday afternoon, the teacher took us to Tiananmen to fly a kite. There are so many kites above the square! There are golden "bees", there are snow-white "swans", and silver-gray "doves". They fly freely and decorate the sky above Tiananmen Square colorfully.
