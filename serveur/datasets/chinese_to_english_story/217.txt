At night in the square When the night falls and the sky is full of stars, the beautiful Bigui Plaza is always lively. On festive nights, the square seems more lively than usual. People came here one after another during the May 1 leisure time. The fountain in the center of the square seems to be spraying higher. Look at the springs it spouts, which are even more brilliant under the mapping of colorful lights. The spring water that comes out is various, some are like fireworks, spraying a thick water mist; some are like water polo, spinning beautiful dances in the air; some are like meteors, and slowly fall down after shooting into the sky .
