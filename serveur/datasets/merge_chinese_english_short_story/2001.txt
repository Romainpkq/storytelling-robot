At daybreak, the bird woke up and sang sternly: "Dewdrops, crystal clear. Like little pearls, hanging on the grass." The sun heard, and said, "What is the dewdrop? Let me see." The sun opened her eyes and looked hard at the grass, but saw nothing. The sun froze, "Oh, where's the dewdrop? Where did it go?" The sun heard it and said, "What is the dewdrop?"
