Ants and bees people always love to praise bees. Ants are very unhappy. One day, the ants crawled to the tree in search of food, and were hitting the bees in the flowers. The ant asked: "Bee, do you say I am hardworking?" The bee said: "You are like me, busy all day." The ant asked again: "Why do people only praise you?" The bee said with a smile: Because your hard work is for yourself, my hard work is for others. "
