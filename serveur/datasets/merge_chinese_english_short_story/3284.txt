One morning early in the morning, the swallows found that there were many small waterdrops on the green grass, which were very beautiful. Swallow invites its friends to see it soon. After a while, many swallows, birds, bees, and butterflies flew. At this time, the sun also rose. Everyone said, "Ah! In the sunshine, the little drops of water are more beautiful!" After a while, the beautiful little drops of water disappeared. Xiao Cao is very sad.
