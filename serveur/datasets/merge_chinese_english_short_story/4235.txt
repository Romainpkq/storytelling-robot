There is a pomegranate tree in the yard. There are many pomegranates hanging on the tree. Mother said, "Pomegranate can be eaten as soon as it smiles." When does pomegranate smile? Kang Kang sang to it, it didn't laugh. Kang Kang danced it, he didn't laugh. Kang Kang told him a joke, but he didn't laugh either. Autumn has come . The pomegranate on the tree slowly showed rows of teeth and smiled. Kang Kang looked at the pomegranate and smiled.
