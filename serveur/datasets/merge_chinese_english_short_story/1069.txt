Kangaroo and gold An old kangaroo takes his child out of the way. When he was tired of walking and put down the child to rest, he found a pile of gold around him. The old kangaroo looked at the golden gold and still put his child in the bag. The grass on the side of the road said to the kangaroo, "Wouldn't you use your sack to fill a bag full of gold?" "No." The kangaroo said, "I still want my child. In my mother's heart, I lost it. Loved ones, gold is worthless. "The old kangaroo looked at Jin Chancan's gold and still put his child in the bag.
