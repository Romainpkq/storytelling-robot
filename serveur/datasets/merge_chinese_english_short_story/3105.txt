In the past, a couple opened a hotel, and the business was very prosperous because of the cheap wines they sold. One day, when the husband went out, the wife secretly mixed water in the wine and sold an extra five yuan. In the evening when the husband came back, the wife proudly told her husband the "secret", thinking she would get a compliment from the husband. Unexpectedly, her husband stomped his feet when he heard it, crying with a headache. While crying, he told his wife that the most valuable thing is to talk about credibility. You sold our most valuable thing with a credibility of only five yuan. Sure enough, since then their business is not as prosperous as before, and finally finally completely bankrupt. When her wife was dying of poverty and she was dying, she shed tears in regret and said, "I understand ..."
