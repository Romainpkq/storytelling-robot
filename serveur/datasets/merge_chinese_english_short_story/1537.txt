Hua Luogeng guessed the book The famous mathematician Hua Luogeng read books differently. He got a book, and instead of reading it from beginning to end, he thought about it for a while, then closed his eyes and meditated. He guessed the layout of the book, and then opened the book after careful consideration. If the author's thinking was consistent with his own guess, he would no longer read it. Hua Luogeng's guessing method not only saves time in reading, but also cultivates his own thinking and imagination, so as not to make himself a slave to books.
