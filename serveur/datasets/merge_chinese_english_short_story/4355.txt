They shed tears in the dense forest where some small animals live, and they live happily. Suddenly one day, a lot of people came from the forest. They sawed down a big tree. The little animals ran around in panic. The panda said: "I have no bamboo to eat." The snake said: "I have no place to hide." The bird said: "I have no place to lay a nest and lay eggs." As they said, they all shed sad tears.
