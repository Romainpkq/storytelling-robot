Little raindrops Little raindrops are a group of cute little dolls. They are sensible and are most willing to help others. If the flowers are thirsty, the raindrops will jump from the sky and let the flowers absorb the rain. If the fish in the lake felt suffocated, they jumped down and made bubbles in the lake water to let the fish breathe.
