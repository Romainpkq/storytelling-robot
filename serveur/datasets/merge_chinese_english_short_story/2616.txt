The lion and the fox The lion is old and unable to catch the beast, so he wants to live by means of tricks. He got into a cave and lay down pretending to be sick. Many beasts came to visit him in the cave and were eaten by him. Seeing his trick, the fox stood at the entrance and said, "Lion King, how is your body?" The lion said, "My body is not good. Why don't you come in?" The fox replied, "Because I see many footprints -There are many holes, but there are no holes, so I will not go in.
