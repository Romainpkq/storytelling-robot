Don't underestimate the tiny fish scales, it's so useful! The scale is the "bone" of the fish exposed outside the body. It is like a fish wearing a piece of armor, which has a protective effect. It can also help the fish prevent bacteria in the water from attacking it. The scales on the back and abdomen are not the same. The back is darker and the abdomen is lighter.
