It was windy. I asked my mother: "What does the wind look like?" Mother shook her head and said, "I don't know. What do you say?" I smiled and said, "The wind is like a hairdresser. When happy, I comb my hair neatly. .When I am angry, I blow my hair in a mess. "My mother heard it, and praised again and again:" Not bad, not bad! Your imagination is really rich. "
