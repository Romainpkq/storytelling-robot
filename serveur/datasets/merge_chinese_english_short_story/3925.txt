How to grow vegetables in winter in winter, the weather is cold, in order to eat vegetables in summer, people build greenhouses for these vegetables. General greenhouses use glass or plastic film as walls and roofs to shield the wind and rain, absorb sunlight, and also need to turn on heating to increase the temperature. If there is not enough light, install an "artificial sun". In this way, despite the number of midwinter days, people can still cultivate summer vegetables.
